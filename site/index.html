What This Query Does
🔗 Step 1 — Token Lookup
Resolves input tokens to active_customer_id via app_compliance.cash.customers
👥 Step 2 — Connected Users
Finds 1st-degree connections via SSN, bank account, payment card, email, and Cash for Teens
💰 Step 3 — Transfers
Cash-in counts (successful & failed) from transfers and cash_ins
📤 Step 4 — P2P Movements
Sent/received counts, unique counterparties from movements and p2p_transactions
₿ Step 5 — BTC Withdrawals
Bitcoin withdrawal transaction counts for each connected user
⚠️ Step 6 — Chargebacks
Chargeback totals from chargebacks table
📋 Step 7 — SAR Cases
Open and closed SAR case tokens from app_sar.sargent.cases
📊 Step 8 — Final Output
Aggregated view: connection types, degrees, all activity metrics, SAR status
